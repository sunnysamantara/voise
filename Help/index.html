  • How to convert speech to text?

    Click on the edit option.Then select start option (ctrl + R) and start speaking.Your speech will be converted to text.

  • How to convert speech to text?

    Start writing on the text editor.Then click on the edit option.Then select check option (ctrl + D).Then your will be converted to text.

  • Speech shortcuts for punctuation mark?

    “.” – Point

    “,” – comma

    “!” – Exclamation mark

    “/” – Slash

    “?” – Question mark

    “@” – At the rate

    “$” – Dollar

    “%” – percent after a number (x percent )

    “=” – Equals

    “-” – Minus

    “+” – Plus

    “#” – Hashtag
